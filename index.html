About Us

Who We Are

The official student body representing the mathematics community at IIT Delhi.

The Mathematics Society (MathSoc) at IIT Delhi is dedicated to nurturing a vibrant mathematical culture. We serve as a dynamic platform connecting students with transformative opportunities in research, problem-solving, and academia beyond the traditional classroom setting.
